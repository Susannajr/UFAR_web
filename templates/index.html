The French University in Armenia (UFAR) is a higher educational institution established in 2000 within the framework of the agreement on cultural, scientific and technical cooperation between the governments of Armenia and France.

The element that distinguishes UFAR is the connection with Europe and the world that the university creates for its students. By obtaining a double diploma - French (European) and Armenian, acquiring international knowledge and being in frequent contact with foreign professors, UFAR students benefit from strong international ties and enter the European reality. International development also comes from the ties that UFAR has with various structures uniting Francophone countries, such as the OIF (International Organization of the Francophonie), the AUF (University Agency of the Francophonie), territorial communities, Francophone agencies, etc. The strong relations between UFAR and its partner universities reinforce UFARâ€™s role as a true gateway to the world.

Faculties

Jean Moulin Lyon 3 University (UJML) and Toulouse 3 Paul Sabatier University are historical academic partners of UFAR.

Edu Programs

Law

To provide fundamental and solid knowledge of Armenian public law, private law, international law, and, starting from the third year, French law. This educational program is organized in collaboration with Jean Moulin Lyon 3 University.

Marketing

To provide fundamental and advanced knowledge and skills in the fields of marketing, sales, management, and decision-making that will meet the standards of national and international professionals. This educational program is organized in partnership with Jean Moulin Lyon 3 University.

Finance

To provide in-depth theoretical knowledge in the field of finance and economics, to prepare specialists in theoretical and practical fields who will specialize as economists, financial managers, banking and insurance specialists. This educational program is organized in collaboration with Jean Moulin Lyon 3 University.

Management

To provide in-depth theoretical knowledge in the field of management and economics, to prepare specialists in theoretical and practical fields who will specialize as economists, managers, and financial managers. This educational program is organized in collaboration with Jean Moulin Lyon 3 University.

Computer Science

To provide in-depth theoretical knowledge of programming, systems, networks, databases, machine architecture, through practical training (which makes up one third of the educational program) to introduce the foundations on which current and future methods, operations and tools are built, to prepare for entry into active life by guiding and providing professional skills. This educational program is organized in partnership with the University of Toulouse 3 Paul Sabatier.

Salva Nakouzi

Armed with its 22 years of experience, the French University in Armenia has created an ambitious educational program based on the programs of the famous French universities Jean-Moulin Lyon 3 and Toulouse 3 Paul Sabatier. Thanks to this cooperation, UFAR students receive two diplomas: French and Armenian. UFAR graduates are Francophone, multilingual, they are experienced professionals in their field, who hold responsible positions in high-level enterprises and administrative structures, create their own enterprises or choose academic activities.

University administration

The Board of Trustees, being the highest administrative and supervisory body, implements the management of the French University in Armenia (UFAR) in accordance with its statutory powers. The Board of Trustees consists of representatives of the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of France and Armenia, the Ministry of Education, Science, Culture and Sports of the Republic of Armenia, Jean Moulin Lyon 3 University, representatives of the associative and private sectors. The Board of Trustees meets twice a year.

The pedagogical and administrative management of the university is carried out by the French-Armenian team led by the Rector of the University of Yerevan, Mrs. Salva Nakouzi. The Management Committee is composed of the Rector, the Vice-Rector, the Director of Administration and Finance, and the Director of Academic Affairs. The meetings of the Management Committee are convened regularly.
